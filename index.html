Interests

I am broadly interested in making robots intelligent through learning and interaction. I deeply believe in the potential for AI and robotics to make a larger than Internet-scale delta on our world. Robots will be found everywhere in our daily lives — from homes to factories, replacing humans in tedious and dangerous tasks.

There are two main problems we are facing right now: improving reasoning for long horizon tasks and improving manipulation for interfacing with the world. I am interested in tackling frontier problems.
