About Me

I'm currently a junior at Stanford University, studying CS. I'm working at as a research intern focusing on robot foundation models. My interests lie in robotics and generative modeling. I deeply believe in human ingenuity and the potential of AI. I am interested in hard problems, big and small.

I previously did research on spatial reasoning evaluations with Sharon Lee and Fan-Yun Sun under Chris Manning. I also researched contact estimation for dexterous robotic hands with Albert Wu under Karen Liu. I worked at Trestle as a SWE intern. I was a software team member of Stanford AUV, where I wrote controls for autonomous underwater vehicles.

I coached and mentored a high school VEX robotics team from 2023-2025. In our final year, we won the world championship! You can click here to watch our 2025 World Championship recap.
